== Description ==
Bidwise helps you increase and maximize revenue per visitor so that you can focus on creating great content. Our high-performing intent-driven native ad units blend in perfectly with your site’s unique layout, making them way more engaging than traditional banner ads. Our powerful exit-intent monetization lets you earn incremental revenue from users that are about to leave your site. And our smart in-text ad placements allow you to generate even more revenue from your content.

We work with more than 35,000 different shopping advertisers in order to deliver the highest value to our publishers.

Our ShopScan technology scans your site content and serves the most relevant ad units and in-text links by matching your sites content with your visitors’ shopping intent in real-time. Bidwise’s engaging platform enjoys some of the highest CTRs in the entire industry. 

Bidwise welcomes WordPress publishers of all sizes to join us in being part of the next generation of online advertising. Sign Up today!

== Installation ==
1. Upload plugin to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
